4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your product data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room database and Android SharedPreferences. As a guest user, your data does not leave your device for storage purposes.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including secure local database storage and encrypted network connections (HTTPS) for any data transmitted to third-party services. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4.3 Data Backup

Android's built-in backup system may include CartNap's local database and preferences in device backups. You can manage this through your device's backup settings. Temporary files and cache data are excluded from backups.

6. Advertising and Consent (GDPR / EEA Users)

CartNap displays advertisements to non-Premium users through Google AdMob. Before serving personalized ads, we collect your consent through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you will be presented with a consent dialog on first launch. You may choose to:

  • Accept personalized ads: AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to show you relevant advertisements.
  • Accept non-personalized ads only: Ads will still be shown but will not be tailored to your interests.
  • Decline: Limited or no ads will be served, depending on regional regulations.

You can modify your consent preferences at any time through your device settings or by reinstalling the app. Premium subscribers do not see ads, and no advertising data is collected for them.
