How It Works

Three steps to spend less, regret less.

01
📤

Share a product link

Found something tempting on Amazon, eBay, Zalando, Vinted, AliExpress, ASOS, Shein and more? Tap the Share button and choose CartNap. The product name, price and image are extracted automatically.

02
😴

Let it nap for 48h

CartNap saves the item and starts a reflection timer. The product goes to sleep — a countdown begins and you can't impulse-click Buy.

03
🧘

Decide with clarity

When it wakes up, you decide: buy it if you still want it — or discard it and save the money. Your wallet keeps score.

Smart Link Detection
