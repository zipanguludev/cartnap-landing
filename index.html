FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is CartNap?

CartNap is a free Android app that helps you stop impulse buying. When you find a product you want to buy, share the link to CartNap instead of checking out. The app puts it to sleep for 48 hours (you can chenge this value) — when it wakes up, you decide whether you still want it.

Which shopping sites are supported?

CartNap supports Amazon, eBay, Zalando, Vinted, AliExpress, Shein, Temu, Etsy, Wallapop, Wish, Unieuro, Mediaworld and many more. It automatically extracts the product name, price, and image from the shared link. You can also add products manually.

Is CartNap free?

Yes, CartNap is completely free to download and use on Google Play. No account is required to get started — just install and share your first product link.

How does it actually help me save money?

CartNap introduces a cooling-off period between the impulse to buy and the actual purchase. Studies show this kind of delay reduces impulse spending by up to 70%. Every item you decide not to buy is logged as a saving, and you get a weekly summary every Monday.

Does CartNap work offline?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. You don't need an internet connection to view your napping products or mark them as bought or discarded.
